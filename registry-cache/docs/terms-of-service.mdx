## 1. Creator Attestations & Content Ownership {#section-1}

When submitting content, you represent and warrant that:

- **(a)** All submitted files and assets are either your original creation or you possess the explicit permission, rights, and appropriate licenses to distribute them. You agree to provide proper attribution as required by any third-party licenses or original creators. 
- **(b)** The submission is made in good faith as a genuine, functional contribution. Because the SBM team consists of volunteers who donate their time to review and vet submissions, joke, troll, or intentionally low-effort submissions are strictly prohibited and will be rejected.
- **(c)** The content must be free from malicious, harmful, or unauthorized code. This includes, but is not limited to, viruses, spyware, trojans, adware, game exploits, or any software designed to compromise a user's device, harvest unauthorized data (including license keys), or disrupt the proper functioning of Subway Builder.
- **(d)** If any part of your submission utilizes generative artificial intelligence (AI), you maintain sole responsibility for the output. You must ensure the AI-generated material does not infringe on third-party intellectual property or violate any section of these terms. 
- **(e)** You retain ownership of your content. However, you grant SBM a worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to host, distribute, modify (for compatibility or archiving purposes), display, and feature your content on the platform and its associated channels.

## 2. Maintenance, Visibility, & Deprecation {#section-2}

To ensure the Registry remains functional and reliable for the community:

- **(a)** Content that enters a non-functioning or broken state (e.g., due to Subway Builder game updates) and remains unaddressed or unpatched by the creator for 90 consecutive days may be hidden, archived, or removed at the discretion of the maintainers. 
- **(b)** The publication of empty "placeholder" pages or non-functioning files intended solely to secure a name or page space is strictly prohibited.

## 3. Platform Moderation & Rights Reservation {#section-3}
The SBM team operates on a principle of fair, transparent moderation, but reserve the absolute right to take the following actions to protect the platform and its community:

- **(a)** The right to remove, restrict, or suspend access to any content that violates these terms, infringes on copyright, or is deemed harmful to the community. 
- **(b)** The right to issue warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans to creators who repeatedly or egregiously violate these terms.
- **(c)** The right to modify these terms at any time. Material changes will be communicated to the community via the [Subway Builder Modded Discord server](https://discord.gg/syG9YHMyeG) (see also [Section 5](#section-5)).

## 4. Dispute Resolution & Plagiarism Claims {#section-4}

We take intellectual property rights and community integrity seriously. If you believe another content creator has stolen, plagiarized, or improperly distributed your work on the Registry, please do not initiate public call-outs or personal arguments. Instead, navigate to our Discord server and DM a SBM admin. We will create a thread that will remain private between you and the SBM team to ensure a fair, objective, and quiet investigation. Please be prepared to provide clear evidence of ownership and details of the alleged infringement when opening the thread.

## 5. Community Communication Channels {#section-5}

To keep the community informed and maintain open lines of communication, the SBM team utilizes the following official channels:

- **(a) Primary Updates (Discord):** The vast majority of day-to-day announcements, policy updates, and community news will be broadcasted via the SBM Discord server. Creators and users are highly encouraged to join to stay informed.
- **(b) [Railyard](https://subwaybuildermodded.com/railyard) App Updates:** Major updates regarding the Railyard app will be published on the [SBM Website](https://subwaybuildermodded.com/) as a secondary, public-facing communication channel.
- **(c) [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Subway-Builder-Modded/registry/issues):** The SBM team will provide technical feedback comments on content submissions to the Registry on GitHub within one week of submission. Creators are expected to address relevant comments and issues pertaining to their submissions in a timely manner.  Issues that have not had any activity from the author for one month will be closed, but authors can re-open them in the future if they later address the feedback.
